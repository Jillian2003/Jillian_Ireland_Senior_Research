Wise Up Wednesday • February 4, 2026

Identity Bingo

Identity Mapping

What to Do:

You’ll complete an Identity Mapping worksheet provided to you.

  • Write down different parts of who you are that shape your identity
  • Reflect on why each part matters to you
  • Think about how these parts show up in your academic, social, or personal life

We’ll complete the worksheet together.

You’ll see an example first to help guide you, then you’ll be invited to share if you feel comfortable.

Sharing is encouraged but never required — you can always pass.

This is a respectful, supportive space. Listen actively and honor each other’s experiences.

Values Mapping

What to Do:

You’ll complete a Values Mapping worksheet provided to you.

  • Identify personal values that matter most to you
  • Reflect on what each value means in your life
  • Consider how these values show up in different areas of your life

We’ll work through this together.

You’ll hear an example first, then you’ll be invited to share one value and a reflection if you’d like.

Sharing is always optional.
